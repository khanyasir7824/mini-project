Our group

Clarins’ story began over 65 years ago, spurred by Jacques Courtin-Clarins’ vision for modern cosmetics that would bring beauty and well-being to all women. He founded Clarins, which went on to become an exceptional entrepreneurial success.

Our skincare and make-up expertise is now recognized worldwide: with our two brands, Clarins and myBlend, we are an ambassador for French cosmetics excellence.
